I’m Shubham Nayak (@digilens), an event, cultural, and street photographer driven by a deep passion for visual storytelling—a journey that began in my first year of college. I started with mobile photography, experimenting with composition techniques like the grid rule and the golden ratio, before moving on to my first camera, a Nikon 200D with a 210mm lens. Covering my college fest for the first time became a turning point, sparking a stronger love for capturing real, meaningful moments.
